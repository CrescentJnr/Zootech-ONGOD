FAQs

How does the Holder benefit?

With the opening of the new warehouse we will have an auction house, provided by Solsuites (JellyPascals). With the staked NFT, you can get our token and can bid on NFTs in the range of 0.2-100 SOL occasionally we might add some expensive NFTs, but we want to benefit everyone so it will not be that common.Besides of that our model is 0% Royalty-proof as we generate our revenue outside the SOL-Ecosystem and we are not dependent on that.

What if we do not mint out?

We do not see this as a problem, as shown above we can host the devices as long as its needed in Europe, before we transport them to Dubai in our new warehouse. There's no threat posed if we do not mint out. Although we might not be listed on certain marketplaces, this will help us to mint out quicker as we will auction the house quickly.

Do we plan to reward our holders for staking?

No, we will host game night, our daily poker games and the Raid2Earn group will be a part of Zootech. We strongly believe in "Community comes first", and what holds the community better together than games and interactions? Lets be honest, Raid2Earn pumps all our bags, this will not be canceled.
